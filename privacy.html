Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 4th February 2026

As & When Admin Services ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your personal data when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information that you voluntarily provide to us via our contact form or email. This typically includes your name, email address, phone number, and any details about your business requirements.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data strictly for business purposes, including:

  • Responding to your enquiries.
  • Providing the administrative and e-commerce services you have requested.
  • Complying with legal and tax obligations.

3. Data Storage and Security

We are registered with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO). We take all reasonable steps to ensure your data is stored securely and is only accessible by authorised personnel.

4. Sharing Your Data

We never sell your personal information. We only share data with third parties (such as cloud storage or accounting software) when it is essential to performing the work you have hired us to do.

5. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to request a copy of the data we hold about you, or to ask us to delete it. Please contact info@aawadmin.co.uk for any data-related requests.
